Timeline of Vermouth

An experiment

In 2013, in an attempt to get a better grasp on the details of vermouth’s history, I began constructing a timeline with every credible date I could lay hands on. The timeline below is a snapshot of where I am as of June 6, 2014.

As wonderful—and occasionally handy—as all this data is, there’s far more of the story in what this timelines doesn’t show. The events herein sketch out only the vaguest of arcs. Moreover, huge factors, such as the role played by wine negociants, are entirely missing the picture.

In other words, take this timeline with a grain of salt.

My intention is to update the timeline periodically. If you have any additions or corrections, please let me know.

—Martin
